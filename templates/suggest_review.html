What do the actions do?
Accept
Adds the term to your vocabulary. It will be included in LLM prompts (describe, retag, summarize) and written into file metadata.
Ignore
Adds the term to the stoplist — permanently excluded from future suggestion runs, even if candidates are reset. Does not enter LLM prompts.
Reject
Dismisses the term without adding it to vocabulary or stoplist. Will not resurface in the next suggestion run. Does not enter LLM prompts.
Correct
Accepts a corrected spelling or form of the term instead. The corrected term is added to vocabulary.
