Accept
Keeps the token in the normalized filename as-is. No rule is added to the KB.
Capture
Extracts the token as a structured field (e.g. a date or project code). The token is replaced by its captured value and excluded from the filename's free-text portion.
Ignore
Adds the token to the stoplist — permanently excluded from normalized filenames and LLM context. Apply to tokens that are genuine noise across all files.
Correct
Replaces the token with a canonical spelling or label. The correction is stored in the KB and applied in all future normalize runs.
Reject
Marks the token as noise. Excluded from normalized filenames but not added to the stoplist — use for tokens that are irrelevant in this corpus but may be valid elsewhere.
